× 4 injured in late-night York City shooting  Four people were injured during a shoot out in York City late Monday night. Police were dispatched shortly before midnight to the 600 block of West Princess Street for a report of shots fired. When they arrived on scene they found four shooting victims, including a juvenile female.  Daquez Palmer, Joseph Ramos, Que’sean Brown, and the juvenile female, all of York, were taken to York Hospital for treatment. Hospital officials say all of their injuries are non-life threatening.  A neighbor tells FOX43 News that he heard 5 or 6 shots go off during the melee.  Police have not identified any suspects as of Tuesday morning.  Anyone with information is asked to contact police at 717-846-1234.
