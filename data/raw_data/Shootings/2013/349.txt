1 killed, 3 wounded in Mission shooting  (07-05) 16:58 PDT SAN FRANCISCO -- A 24-year-old man was killed and three other men were injured in an early morning shooting Friday outside a bar in San Francisco's Mission District, authorities said.  Police said the assailant approached a group, pulled out a handgun and opened fire outside Double Dutch, a bar near 16th and Guerrero streets around 1:30 a.m.  Keith Smith of San Francisco was shot in the upper body and abdomen and was pronounced dead at San Francisco General Hospital, according to the city medical examiner's office.  A 22-year-old man was shot in the lower leg, and two 23-year-old men were grazed by bullets, police said. Each is expected to survive.  The suspect ran away toward 15th Street, and no arrests have been made, police said.
