Police are investigating a shooting at a birthday party in Hamilton Township, New Jersey that left one teenager dead and four others wounded.  According to Mercer County First Assistant Prosecutor Angelo Onofri, a total of five teenagers were shot, one of them fatally at a house party in Hamilton Township.  Onofri said Hamilton police received a call about gunshots on the 1100 block of Deutz Road just after 11:00 p.m. Friday night.  Police say the teens were attending a birthday party for a 15-year-old resident of the home. They say approximately 100 people were in attendance, including an adult who was collecting admission at the door.  Police believe the shooting took place inside the living room. The Mercer County Prosecutors office says there have been no arrests at this time and no one is in custody, but this is still a very active police investigation.
