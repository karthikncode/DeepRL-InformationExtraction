Four people were shot during an incident in southeast Washington Saturday night and police are searching for two men in connection with the crime.  Metropolitan police said the four victims were taken to the hospital with non-life-threatening injuries. Two of the victims were juveniles.  The shooting took place in the 2700 block of Stanton Road in southeast Washington.  Police are searching for a man, 6’3” tall, dark completion and long dreadlocks. He has a slight beard and was wearing blue jeans, polo shirt and Nike Foamposite shoes.  The second suspect is a man with light completion, tattoos on one or both arms, wearing blue jeans and white shoes.
