(Photo: WFAA)  Fort Worth police are investigating the shooting of five people in southwest Fort Worth on Tuesday evening.  The victims were sprayed with shotgun pellets around 6 o'clock in the 1700 block of Southcrest Drive.  All of the victims were adults; no one was seriously injured. Two who were struck refused treatment at the scene and three others were taken to John Peter Smith Hospital for treatment of minor injuries.
