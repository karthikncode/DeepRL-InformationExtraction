One killed, four injured in Dillon County shooting (Image 1)Captain Cliff Arnette with the Dillon County Sheriff's Office confirms one person was killed and four people were injured during a shooting Saturday morning in Latta. According to Dillon County Coroner, Donnie Grimsley, Damien Simpson, 21, from Dillon county was killed in the shooting. He also said that one of the injured victims is in critical condition at a local hospital; the other two are in stable condition. Captain Arnette said they now have two suspects in custody; Devante Mcneil, 21, and Benjamin Bethea, 19, are being charged with murder.
