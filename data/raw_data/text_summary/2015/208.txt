1 dead, 4 injured in shooting on Sobota Circle Posted: Sunday, August 30, 2015 10:04 AM EDT Updated: Sunday, August 30, 2015 3:26 PM EDTOne person is dead and four others are injured after an early morning shooting in Southwest Memphis Sunday. The shooting happened in the 3300 block of Sobota Circle in the Richmond Place Apartments. First responders pronounced one person dead on the scene. Another person was taken to the hospital in extremely critical condition. If you have any information that could help police, you are encouraged to call CrimeStoppers at 901-528-CASH.
