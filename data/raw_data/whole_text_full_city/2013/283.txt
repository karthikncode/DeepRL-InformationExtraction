UPDATE: Teenager dead in overnight Dodds Ave. shooting Posted: Sunday, June 23, 2013 11:24 AM EDT Updated: Sunday, June 23, 2013 3:45 PM EDT  A teenager is dead and three other men injured after an early morning shooting Sunday.  Sgt. Wayne Jefferson tells Channel 3 four men were shot at 3:40 a.m. at a laundromat in the 1500 block of Dodds Avenue.  The four victims went to the hospital where police say 19-year-old Demetrius Davis died from his injuries. The other three men have non-life threatening injuries.  The victims tell police they were sitting in a car at 1501 Dodds Ave. when someone started shooting.  Sgt. Jefferson says the suspect is believed to be a light-skinned black male wearing a blue shirt. No other suspect description is available at this time.
