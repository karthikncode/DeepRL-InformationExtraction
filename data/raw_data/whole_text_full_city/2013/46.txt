1 dead, 4 hurt in Vallejo shooting VALLEJO  Photo: Google Maps Image 1 of / 1 Caption Close Image 1 of 1 800 block of Humboldt Street, Vallejo, CA 800 block of Humboldt Street, Vallejo, CA Photo: Google Maps 1 dead, 4 hurt in Vallejo shooting 1 / 1 Back to Gallery  A man was killed and four other people were hurt in a shooting at a home in Vallejo, police said Tuesday.  Oscar Garcia, 22, of Vallejo was killed in the shooting, which happened on the 800 block of Humboldt Street at 8:24 p.m. Monday, police said.  When they arrived, officers found Garcia dead and another man wounded. Police then learned that three other people, a man and two women, had been shot at the home and were already being treated at hospitals, authorities said.  Their wounds are not considered life-threatening, police said.  The injured victims' names weren't released, and police have made no arrests.
