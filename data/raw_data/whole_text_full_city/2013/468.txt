One killed, four injured in Dillon County shooting (Image 1)  Captain Cliff Arnette with the Dillon County Sheriff's Office confirms one person was killed and four people were injured during a shooting Saturday morning in Latta.  According to Dillon County Coroner, Donnie Grimsley, Damien Simpson, 21, from Dillon county was killed in the shooting. His autopsy is being performed on Saturday and the results will be released later this week.  Captain Arnette said they now have two suspects in custody; Devante Mcneil, 21, and Benjamin Bethea, 19, are being charged with murder. Both men are from Latta.  He also said that one of the injured victims is in critical condition at a local hospital; the other two are in stable condition.  He says it happened between 2:30 and 2:45 Saturday morning at The Legion.  Count on News 13 to keep you covered as we receive details on this ongoing investigation.
