AVONDALE, AZ - Authorities are investigating a shooting that injured four people at an Avondale apartment complex Sunday.  Authorities said two people are in critical condition after shots were fired at the Versante Luxury Apartments near 111th Avenue and Van Buren Street.  The conditions of the two other victims were not immediately known.  Police said they do not have any information about a suspect, or possible suspects. They also said no one is in custody.  Police are continuing to investigate.
