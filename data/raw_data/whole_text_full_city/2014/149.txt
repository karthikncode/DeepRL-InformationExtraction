Five men were shot during an altercation in northeast Atlanta Tuesday morning.  Police say the shooting took place on Auburn Avenue near Bell Street around 5 a.m.  Police said a group of about 15 people was gambling when an argument led to the shooting.  Three of the men suffered non-life-threatening injuries and another was taken to the hospital in critical condition. The fifth man was pronounced dead at Grady Memorial Hospital, police said.  He has been identified as Selemon Belai, 27, of Atlanta.
