Several shot at Las Vegas wedding reception Saturday Posted: Sunday, February 16, 2014 1:50 PM EST Updated: Sunday, April 27, 2014 2:31 PM EDT  Crime scene tape surrounds the parking lot of a strip mall Feb. 16, 2014, after a shooting. (Arron Healy/FOX5)  Several people were shot at a wedding reception in Las Vegas on Saturday night, according to police.  The shooting happened about 11:30 p.m. at the El Palacio Del Sol banquet hall near the intersection of Sahara Avenue and Maryland Parkway.  A spokesman for Las Vegas Metro police at the scene told FOX5 that as many as five people were wounded in the shooting. He said the injuries appeared not to be life-threatening.  The spokesman also said the gunman had been arrested.  The shooting is under investigation, police said.    Copyright 2014 KVVU (KVVU Broadcasting Corporation). All rights reserved.
