1 dead, 4 injured in shooting on Sobota Circle Posted: Sunday, August 30, 2015 10:04 AM EDT Updated: Sunday, August 30, 2015 3:26 PM EDT  One person is dead and four others are injured after an early morning shooting in Southwest Memphis Sunday.  The shooting happened in the 3300 block of Sobota Circle in the Richmond Place Apartments.  First responders pronounced one person dead on the scene. Another person was taken to the hospital in extremely critical condition. Three others were taken to the hospital in non-critical condition.  No arrests have been made. This is an ongoing investigation.  If you have any information that could help police, you are encouraged to call CrimeStoppers at 901-528-CASH.  Copyright 2015 WMC Action News 5. All rights reserved.
