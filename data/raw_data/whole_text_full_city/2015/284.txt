Arrest warrants issued for suspects in Houma quadruple shooting Updated: Thursday, October 29, 2015 2:43 PM EDT  Arrest warrants were issued for two men wanted in connection to a Houma shooting that left one person dead and three others wounded.  The Houma Police Department issued warrants for 21-year-old Elward Williams and 24-year-old Anthony Sylvester.  Sylvester, who was initially identified Wednesday as a shooting victim, remain hospitalized. Upon his release, he will be charged with 2nd degree murder, attempted 2nd degree murder and possession of a firearm by a convicted felon.  Williams is wanted for 2nd degree murder and possession of a firearm by a convicted felon.  A 19-year-old male who was shot in the shoulder was released from the hospital after receiving treatment. A 25-year-old who was shot in the face remains hospitalized in critical condition.  Copyright 2015 WVUE. All rights reserved.
